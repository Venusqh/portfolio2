Database Management

Organizing, storing, and maintaining data efficiently to ensure accessibility, consistency, and security across various applications.

Stakeholder Contact Data Integration & Matching

During the AWS mailout process, I identified challenges in efficiently linking stakeholder contact information to affected spatial features. Working collaboratively with staff, I consolidated district-wide contact lists and used MS Access to perform relational table joins between contact records and spatial datasets. This established a repeatable and scalable matching workflow that achieved over 70% automated match success, significantly reducing manual data handling during a time-sensitive process and improving data accuracy and reliability for mailout preparation.

Public Tree Inventory Database

I contributed to a city-wide public tree survey initiative as part of a team effort. We developed an accessible online survey platform that allowed community members to submit valuable tree data across the region. After data collection using ArcGIS Survey123, I took the lead in processing, converting, and organizing the submissions into a structured and reliable database using MS Access. I also performed quality control checks to ensure data accuracy and consistency throughout the project.
